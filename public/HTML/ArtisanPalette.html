About Artisan Palette

Inspiring creativity since 2010

Welcome to Artisan Palette

Founded in 2010, Artisan Palette has been the premier destination for professional and hobbyist artists seeking the finest art supplies. Our passion for creativity and commitment to quality has made us a trusted partner in the artistic community.

Our Mission

To inspire and empower artists at every level by providing access to premium art supplies, expert knowledge, and exceptional service. We believe that great art begins with great materials.

Our Story

What started as a small local art supply store has grown into a comprehensive online destination for artists worldwide. We understand that every stroke of the brush, every pencil mark, and every color choice matters in creating your masterpiece.

Refund Policy

Returns and refund information

Last updated: January 2025

30-Day Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. To be eligible for a return, items must be:

  • Unused and in original condition
  • In original packaging with all accessories
  • Returned within 30 days of purchase

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom or personalized products
  • Opened paint sets or liquid mediums
  • Items damaged by misuse
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective)

Return Process

To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA)
  3. Pack items securely with original packaging
  4. Ship to our returns center using provided label

Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your return:

  • Approved refunds processed within 5-7 business days
  • Refunds issued to original payment method
  • Shipping costs are non-refundable (unless item was defective)

Exchanges

We offer exchanges for defective items or wrong items shipped. Contact us immediately if you receive a damaged or incorrect item.
