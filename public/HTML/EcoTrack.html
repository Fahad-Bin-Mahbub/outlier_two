Quick Energy Saving Tips

Adjust Your Thermostat

Set your thermostat to 68°F in winter and 78°F in summer. Each degree can save 1-3% on energy costs.

Unplug Phantom Loads

Electronics continue to draw power when plugged in but turned off. Use power strips to fully disconnect devices.

Use Cold Water for Laundry

Washing clothes in cold water can reduce energy usage by up to 90% while still getting clothes clean.

LED Lighting

Replace incandescent bulbs with LEDs to use up to 75% less energy and last 25 times longer.

Run Full Loads

Only run dishwashers and washing machines when they're full to maximize efficiency.
