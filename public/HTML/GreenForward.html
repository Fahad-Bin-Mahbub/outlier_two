Reforesting Our Planet's Future

Together, we're planting hope, restoring ecosystems, and fighting climate change one tree at a time. Join our mission to create a sustainable future for generations to come.

The Crisis We Face

Every year, our planet loses 5.5 million hectares of tropical forest. That's equivalent to losing a forest the size of Costa Rica annually.

Deforestation contributes to 11% of global CO₂ emissions, accelerating climate change and threatening the homes of countless species.

1.6 billion people worldwide depend on forests for their livelihoods, yet these vital ecosystems continue to disappear at an alarming rate.

The urgent reality of deforestation worldwide

Our Mission to Heal the Earth

GreenForward is dedicated to large-scale reforestation and forest restoration worldwide. We work with local communities to plant native species, restore degraded landscapes, and create sustainable forest ecosystems.

Climate Action

Trees are nature's most effective carbon capture technology. Our reforestation efforts can sequester up to 23.4 billion tons of CO₂ over 30 years.

Biodiversity

Restoring habitat for millions of species while creating corridors that connect fragmented ecosystems and protect endangered wildlife.

Communities

Creating sustainable livelihoods for local communities through eco-friendly jobs, supporting 2,000+ people annually while protecting forests.
