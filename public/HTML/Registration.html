Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our registration process and courses

Our registration process is simple and user-friendly. First, browse available courses and select the one you're interested in. Complete the multi-step registration form with your personal information, course details, and payment information. Review all information before submitting your registration. Once complete, you'll receive a confirmation email with access instructions for your course.

We accept various payment methods for your convenience, including credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are processed securely, and we never store your complete card information. If you need alternative payment arrangements, please contact our support team.

Yes, you can register for multiple courses in a single registration process. During the course selection step, simply check the option to add more courses. You'll be able to add additional courses before finalizing your registration. This streamlined approach saves you time and allows you to plan your learning journey more effectively.

We understand that plans can change. If you need to modify or cancel your registration, please contact our support team at least 7 days before the course start date for a full refund. Cancellations made within 7 days of the start date may be eligible for a partial refund or course credit. You can also transfer your registration to another course or future session in many cases.

Yes, our registration form is designed with accessibility in mind. We follow WCAG guidelines to ensure users with disabilities can navigate and complete the form without barriers. The form works with screen readers, supports keyboard navigation, provides clear error messages, and maintains appropriate color contrast. If you encounter any accessibility issues, please contact our support team for immediate assistance.
