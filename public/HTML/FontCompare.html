About FontPairer

FontPairer was created to help designers, developers, and content creators make better typography decisions. Our tool simplifies the process of finding perfect font combinations while ensuring accessibility standards are met.

Whether you're designing a website, creating marketing materials, or working on any visual project, FontPairer helps you compare fonts side by side and check contrast ratios for better readability.

Getting Started with FontPairer

Welcome to FontPairer! This guide will help you make the most of our font comparison tool.

Step 1: Select Your Fonts

Step 2: Customize Colors

Step 3: Fine-tune Typography
