Frequently Asked Questions

Registration & Tickets

What's included in my registration?

All registrations include access to keynotes, sessions, expo area, and networking events. Workshops may require additional registration. VIP tickets include additional perks like speaker dinner access and VIP lounge access.

Is there a virtual attendance option?

Yes, we offer a virtual pass that gives you access to livestreams of all keynotes and selected sessions. Virtual attendees can participate in Q&A and networking through our virtual platform.

Can I transfer my ticket to someone else?

Yes, tickets can be transferred up to 7 days before the event. Please contact our support team at support@techvision.com to process the transfer.

Venue & Accommodations

Where is the conference located?

TechVision 2025 will take place at the TechVision Innovation Center, 123 Silicon Valley Blvd, San Francisco, CA 94105, USA.

Is there a discounted rate at partner hotels?

Yes, we've negotiated special rates with several nearby hotels. You'll receive booking information with your registration confirmation.

Program & Content

How can I become a speaker?

Our call for speakers is currently closed for 2025. Please check back in October 2025 for speaking opportunities at our next event.

Will presentations be available after the event?

Yes, presentations will be available to all attendees through our event platform for up to 6 months after the conference.

How can I become a sponsor?

Contact our sponsorship team at sponsors@techvision.com for information about our various sponsorship packages. We offer options for companies of all sizes.
