Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new task?

Click the "Add New" button in the Schedule section, fill in the form with your task details, and click "Save Item".

Can I set reminders for events?

Yes, reminders can be set when creating or editing an event by specifying the start time, with notifications planned for future updates.

Is there a mobile app available?

Currently, Scheduler Pro is web-based and optimized for mobile browsers. A dedicated app is in development.
