Expert Design Tips

Space Planning

Begin with the focal point of your room and work outward. Leave adequate pathways between furniture pieces (at least 30 inches for walkways).

Color Theory

Use the 60-30-10 rule: 60% dominant color, 30% secondary color, and 10% accent color to create a balanced space.

Lighting

Incorporate three types of lighting: ambient (overall illumination), task (focused light for specific activities), and accent (highlighting features).
